Help & Support

Are you stuck and need help? Mtaani mart got you

How to Register or Log In

To start shopping, you need an account. Click Register in the navigation bar to create one, or Login if you already have an account. Once logged in, you can add items to your cart and place orders.

How to Place an Order

Once items are in your cart, go to the Cart page and click “Checkout.” Enter your phone number in 2547XXXXXXXX format, then click “Pay Now.” You’ll receive an M-Pesa STK push prompt to confirm payment.

How to Add an Item to Cart

Browse products on the Products page. Click the “Add to Cart” button under the item you want. Your cart will update automatically, and you can view it anytime by clicking Cart in the navigation bar.

How to Manage Your Cart

You can view your cart anytime by clicking Cart in the navigation bar. From there, you can update quantities, remove items, or proceed to checkout.
